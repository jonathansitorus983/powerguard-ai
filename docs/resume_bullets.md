# Resume Bullets

- Built an AI infrastructure analytics platform that forecasted GPU power demand using utilization, temperature, workload queue, and energy pricing telemetry to support compute-cost planning.
- Deployed a FastAPI prediction service and Streamlit executive dashboard to translate real-time GPU telemetry into power, cost, and cooling-risk insights.
- Developed anomaly and spike detection logic to flag high-risk AI workload periods, improving visibility into operational reliability and energy usage.
