# PowerGuard AI Project Pitch

PowerGuard AI is a predictive analytics platform for AI infrastructure teams. It forecasts GPU power demand, estimates short-term energy cost, and flags cooling-risk events before they become operational issues.

## Business Analytics Angle
The project connects technical telemetry to business outcomes: cost forecasting, capacity planning, operational reliability, and infrastructure decision support.

## MIS Angle
The project acts as an enterprise decision-support system that collects operational data, models infrastructure behavior, and visualizes insights for technical and business stakeholders.
